Design direction D

Calm

Money is stressful — this direction stays quiet and reassuring. Generous whitespace, large calm numbers on frosted glass, and a single narrow column keep attention on one thing at a time. Adding an expense is a gentle guided wizard, not a dense form.

Made for households and roommates who want clarity and low anxiety over power-user density — and it works the same on a phone or a laptop.

Ground Ink Soft teal accent Owed Owes

Key tradeoffs

Calm over dense. One centered column and lots of air mean less on screen at once — power users who want everything visible may find it slow. Guided over fast. The add-expense wizard reduces anxiety and errors but costs an extra tap or two versus a single mega-form. Understatement over pop. Semantic color is desaturated so nothing shouts, which trades a little glanceability for a lower-stress feel.
